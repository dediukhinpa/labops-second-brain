# Publisher Pitch Meeting — 2026-03-12

## Summary

This raw note from the publisher meeting is what triggered the Q2-lock decision two weeks later — follow the link to see how a vault entry compounds over time.

Thirty-minute introductory call with a publisher who was warm and specifically positive about Tideborn's turn-based combat and art direction. They want to see a forty-minute playable vertical slice including the tutorial, with a soft deadline of end of Q2 (June). Deal structure floated was a recoupable advance against royalties at a range that would extend the studio's runway by six to twelve months. Follow-up call scheduled in roughly two weeks; we need to be back in touch by 2026-03-26.

- Vertical slice target: 40 minutes of gameplay, tutorial included, ready by 2026-06-30.
- Marketing-presentable visual quality required by 2026-07-15 (screenshots and trailer-ready).
- Decision on whether to engage this publisher exclusively must be made by 2026-03-31, before the follow-up call.
